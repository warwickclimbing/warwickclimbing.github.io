Who Are We

Warwick Climbing Club are a group of outdoor minded people interested in rock climbing, hill walking, scrambling, mountaineering and mountain biking. The club have approximately 100 members drawn from Warwick, Leamington Spa, Stratford-upon-Avon, Coventry and surrounding areas. We currently meet twice during the week, Wednesdays at the St Nicholas Park Leisure Centre climbing wall, which is followed by a social meet at the New Bowling Green pub and Thursdays at the University of Warwick Climbing Wall. The club organizes a variety of trips and socials throughout the year; details of this years trips can be found below.

Where To Find Us:

St Nics Climbing Wall:

Wednesdays between 19:00 and 21:30.
Although the wall is in the Sports Centre we [the club] run it. If you want to come and see us at the wall just ask at the Leisure Centre reception and they will point you in the right direction.

New Bowling Green [Pub]:

After climbing on Wednesdays, there is usually a club member present from 21:30. This is the ideal venue to come and say hello and quiz club members about the club and what it does.

University of Warwick Climbing Wall:

Thursdays between 19:00 and 22:00.
